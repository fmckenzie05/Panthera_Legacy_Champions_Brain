# AI_SECURITY_TRENDS

Trends in the AI-security market that should inform [[Anomalie Grid]] positioning and architecture.

Parent: [[WORLD_VIEW]]
See also: [[Anomalie Grid]], [[WORLDVIEW]]

## Trend list

### SOC automation / Tier-1 displacement

- **What:** AI assistants taking over Tier-1 SOC triage.
- **Why it matters:** [[The Oracle]] competes here. Position not as a replacement-for-analysts but as enrichment + prioritization.
- **Status:** adopting framing.

### Detection-as-code and behavioral analytics

- **What:** Detection logic versioned like software; behavioral models replacing static signatures.
- **Why it matters:** [[Nebuchadnezzar]] sits on this trend. Honeynet-derived behavioral data ([[The Construct]]) is a moat.
- **Status:** core to flagship.

### Identity-centric threat detection

- **What:** Identity (and non-human identity) becoming the new perimeter.
- **Why it matters:** [[Sentinel Swarm]] should ingest identity signals, not just network. Likely a near-term product investment.
- **Status:** watching, plan to adopt.

### Honeynets and deception 2.0

- **What:** AI-generated deception assets, dynamic decoys, attacker-in-loop intelligence.
- **Why it matters:** [[The Construct]] is a direct play on this. Most competitors talk passive monitoring; deception is a wedge.
- **Status:** core differentiator.

### LLM-on-LLM threats (prompt injection, model abuse)

- **What:** Attacks targeting LLM-powered systems themselves.
- **Why it matters:** Adjacent product surface — Intellusia could plausibly extend into AI-system security. Not flagship, but worth a capability note.
- **Status:** watching.

### Multi-tenant data isolation and sovereignty

- **What:** Buyers demanding strict tenant-scoped data, region-locked, with auditable boundaries.
- **Why it matters:** [[The Architect]] and tenant model in [[CLIENT_CONNECTION]] must lead with this. It is a procurement gate.
- **Status:** must-have.

### FedRAMP and gov AI compliance

- **What:** Federal compliance regimes for AI systems handling government data.
- **Why it matters:** Connects [[GOVERNMENT_CONTRACTING_PIPELINE_STRATEGY]] to [[Anomalie Grid]]. A long-term moat if pursued.
- **Status:** watching, requires capacity decision.

### XDR consolidation and platform fatigue

- **What:** Buyers consolidating EDR/SIEM/SOAR into XDR platforms.
- **Why it matters:** [[Anomalie Grid]] is not an XDR; pitch as the AI-reasoning layer that augments, not replaces.
- **Status:** affects positioning.

### Threat-informed AI (MITRE ATT&CK alignment)

- **What:** Mapping detections and reasoning to ATT&CK TTPs.
- **Why it matters:** [[The Oracle]] outputs should speak ATT&CK natively. It's the lingua franca of buyer SOCs.
- **Status:** required for credibility.

## How to use

When [[Anomalie Grid]] copy or architecture is being updated, scan this list. Anything marked `core differentiator` or `must-have` should be visible in the buyer-facing pitch in [[VOICE_AND_STYLE]] and [[MEMORY]].
