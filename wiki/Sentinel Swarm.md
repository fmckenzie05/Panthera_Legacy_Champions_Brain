# Sentinel Swarm

Collector and telemetry ingestion node inside [[Anomalie Grid]].

## Description

Sentinel Swarm is the collection layer of [[Anomalie Grid]].
It represents the distributed sensors and telemetry inputs that feed the rest of the product.

## Why it matters

It is the entry point for real-world data in the platform architecture.
