# Renewal And Expansion

How a client renews and grows. Retention compounds — losing a client is far more expensive than landing a new one.

Parent: [[CLIENT_CONNECTION]]
See also: [[Client Journey]], [[Buyer Personas]]

## Renewal philosophy

A renewal is not a sales event. It is the natural consequence of the client seeing measurable value every quarter. If renewal feels like a fight, retention failed two quarters earlier.

## Renewal signals (leading indicators)

- Quarterly briefings are attended by the CISO/Director, not just the SOC lead.
- Client champion forwards Intellusia output internally without prompting.
- Alert volume from [[The Oracle]] is decreasing while severity-weighted findings are increasing — i.e., signal is improving.
- Integrations expand (more SIEM/SOAR connections, more identity sources).

If those signals are absent, intervene 90 days before renewal — not 30.

## Expansion paths

### More environments

- Additional production environments, geographic regions, or subsidiary tenants.
- Lowest friction, fastest revenue.

### More telemetry depth

- Adding identity, endpoint, cloud-config telemetry to existing [[Sentinel Swarm]] coverage.
- Lifts [[The Oracle]]'s effectiveness; increases usage-based revenue.

### More users / seats

- Expanding [[Zion]] access to more analyst teams.
- Modest revenue, big stickiness gain.

### Adjacent products

- When future Intellusia capabilities ship (e.g., AI-system security per [[AI_SECURITY_TRENDS]]).
- Long-horizon expansion, not first-year.

### MCP / agent-tool surface

- Exposing [[Anomalie Grid]] capabilities as tools to client AI agents.
- Future surface; see [[Connection Surfaces]] and [[SAAS_TRENDS]].

## Churn risks

- Client champion leaves — re-onboarding the new champion is mandatory, not optional.
- Procurement consolidation pressure — pre-empt by being the AI-reasoning layer that augments, not replaces, the XDR per [[AI_SECURITY_TRENDS]].
- Eval drift — if [[The Oracle]] quality regresses unmeasured, churn follows. See [[AI_DESIGN_PATTERNS]] eval discipline.

## What good retention looks like

- Net revenue retention > 120% across the cohort.
- Renewal conversations are calendar items, not negotiations.
- Expansion conversations begin no later than month 6 of the contract.
