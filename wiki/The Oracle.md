# The Oracle

Agentic reasoning and prediction node inside [[Anomalie Grid]].

The Oracle is the reasoning and threat-prediction layer of [[Anomalie Grid]].
It sits above inference outputs and turns signals into predictions, prioritization, and recommended next steps.
