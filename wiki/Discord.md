# Discord

Community and communication connector for **ScriptLabs Studios**.

## Role

- Community and team communication surface
- Automation target for alerts, notifications, and bot interactions
- Distribution channel for studio announcements and audience engagement

## Typical workflows

- Status alerts from [[ScripLabStudio8]] automation jobs
- Team/creator network coordination
- Inbound inquiry bot triggers
- Cross-post content from [[YouTube]] clips

## Connection notes

- Bot credentials kept outside vault in secret store referenced by [[ScripLabStudio8]]
