# MVFN — Moral Vale Foundation Network

**MVFN** is the ethics, morality, consulting, and education platform affiliated with Intellusia I3. It operates as a distinct brand entity — married to Intellusia's organizational infrastructure — giving the founder creative space to work with educators, creators, and purpose-driven collaborators outside the technical product track.

## Summary

MVFN (Moral Vale Foundation Network) is a values-anchored platform for ethics consulting, moral education, and creator collaboration. It is structurally connected to Intellusia I3 but operates as its own brand, allowing cross-disciplinary work without conflating it with the AI-security or studio delivery tracks.

## What MVFN does

- **Ethics and moral consulting** — advisory work grounded in principled frameworks
- **Education** — curriculum, content, and learning experiences on values-driven topics
- **Creator collaboration** — platform for working with other creators and thinkers outside the tech-product lane

## Why it connects to Intellusia

MVFN is "married" to Intellusia I3 in the organizational hierarchy — not a subsidiary, but a connected affiliate entity. This structure:

- Keeps Intellusia's IP and product focus clean (Anomalie Grid, ScriptLabs delivery, GovCon)
- Gives the founder room to work creatively with educators, artists, and creators
- Expands the brand surface without diluting the technical flagship narrative
- Creates a values/ethics dimension that naturally complements AI-security work (ethics of AI, responsible tech, digital rights)

## How MVFN fits the hierarchy

```
Intellusia I3 (hierarchy head)

├── GovCon / Azure / Babel ← supporting capabilities
```
