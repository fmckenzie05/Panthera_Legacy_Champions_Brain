# ScripLabStudio8

## Summary

**ScriptLabs Studios** (domain: scriptstudio8.com) is the client-facing brand of [[Intellusia Studios]]. It is an AI-powered development studio co-founded in 2018 by **Honorine Ndom Ndzah** (CEO) and **Fernando A McKenzie** (CIO). It delivers web, mobile, Web3/blockchain, AI-powered SaaS, consulting, and support services. The site is a Next.js 15 / React 19 application deployed at scriptstudio8.com.
