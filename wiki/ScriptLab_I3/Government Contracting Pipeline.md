# Government Contracting Pipeline

Government data and opportunity pipeline node for Intellusia Studios.

## Description

This note represents the government-contracting data pipeline capability inside Intellusia Studios.
It covers the extraction, cleaning, enrichment, and export of opportunity and award data.

## Why it matters

This node broadens the company beyond cyber product work and supports research, lead generation, and delivery workflows.

## Product relevance — Anomalie Grid

When the pipeline surfaces cybersecurity-related opportunities (network defense, threat detection, SOC tooling, defense-adjacent IT), [[Anomalie Grid]] is the natural product to position. Government and defense clients operating SOCs or managing network security are a valid target audience for the platform.

This connection is **secondary** — the pipeline's primary job is market intelligence and opportunity capture, not Anomalie Grid sales. But if an opportunity aligns with AI-powered threat detection or network visibility, Anomalie Grid becomes part of the response.

Relevant opportunity signals to watch for:
- Cybersecurity monitoring and detection contracts
- SOC tooling or SIEM replacement at civilian agencies or defense contractors
- Network visibility and anomaly detection requirements
- AI/ML applied to cyber (Morpheus + Azure credibility is an asset here)
