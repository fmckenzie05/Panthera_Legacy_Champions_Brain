## What this relationship means

Intellusia Studios is the **mother company** — the legal entity, the product R&D owner, and the strategic layer.

ScriptLab Studio 8 (**ScriptLab_I3**) is the **client-facing brand** — the public face that clients see, hire, and interact with. It handles web, mobile, and Web3 delivery powered by the Hermes agentic stack.

Clients experience ScriptLab_I3. Intellusia holds the architecture.

## Why this relationship matters

This separation allows:
- **Clean brand positioning** — ScriptLab_I3 is sharp, accessible, and market-facing without the complexity of the full Intellusia platform story
- **Protected strategic layer** — Anomalie Grid, GovCon work, and Intellusia's broader R&D stays under the parent brand
- **Agentic leverage** — The Hermes stack behind ScriptLab_I3 is an Intellusia capability, not a public differentiator
